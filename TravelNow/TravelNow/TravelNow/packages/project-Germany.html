Germany: 8 Days, 7 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Berlin

Arrive in Berlin and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Brandenburg Gate and the Reichstag building. Take a stroll along Unter den Linden and enjoy a dinner of traditional German cuisine at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Berlin

Discover the historical and cultural landmarks of Berlin. Visit the Berlin Wall Memorial and Checkpoint Charlie. Explore Museum Island and visit the Pergamon Museum. In the evening, take a leisurely walk along the East Side Gallery.

Day 3: Berlin to Dresden

Travel to Dresden by train. Check into your hotel and explore the historic center of Dresden, including the Zwinger Palace and Dresden Frauenkirche. Visit the Dresden Castle and enjoy views of the Elbe River. In the evening, dine at a local beer garden.

Day 4: Dresden to Nuremberg

Travel to Nuremberg and check into your hotel. Visit the Nuremberg Castle and explore the Old Town, including the Church of Our Lady (Frauenkirche) and the Albrecht DÃ¼rer House. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Nuremberg bratwurst dinner.

Day 5: Nuremberg to Munich

Travel to Munich and check into your hotel. Visit the Marienplatz and see the Glockenspiel. Explore the Residenz Museum and the English Garden. In the evening, dine at a traditional Bavarian beer hall.

Day 6: Munich

Take a day trip to Neuschwanstein Castle, a fairytale castle nestled in the Bavarian Alps. Explore the castle and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Return to Munich in the evening and visit the Viktualienmarkt for dinner.

Day 7: Munich to Heidelberg

Travel to Heidelberg and check into your hotel. Visit Heidelberg Castle and explore the Old Town, including the Karl Theodor Bridge and Church of the Holy Spirit. In the evening, take a walk along the Philosopher's Path.

Day 8: Departure from Frankfurt

Spend your final morning in Heidelberg shopping for souvenirs on Hauptstrasse or visiting any last-minute sights. Head to Frankfurt for your departure, taking with you memories of Germany's rich history, culture, and cuisine.
