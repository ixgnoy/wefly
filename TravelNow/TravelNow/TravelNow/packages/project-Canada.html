Canada : 10 Days, 9 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Vancouver

Arrive in Vancouver and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to Stanley Park and the Vancouver Aquarium. In the evening, enjoy dinner at a waterfront restaurant in Coal Harbour.

Day 2: Vancouver

Discover the highlights of Vancouver. Visit Granville Island in the morning, then head to the Capilano Suspension Bridge Park for an afternoon of adventure. In the evening, enjoy a performance at the Vancouver Playhouse.

Day 3: Whistler

Take a day trip to Whistler. Enjoy the scenic Sea-to-Sky Highway on the way. Spend the day exploring Whistler Village, taking a ride on the Peak 2 Peak Gondola, and enjoying outdoor activities. Return to Vancouver in the evening.

Day 4: Vancouver to Banff

Fly to Calgary and transfer to Banff. Check into your hotel and visit the Banff Gondola for a breathtaking view of the Rockies. In the evening, explore Banff Avenue and dine at a local restaurant.

Day 5: Banff National Park

Spend the day exploring Banff National Park. Visit Lake Louise and Moraine Lake, take a hike, or enjoy a canoe ride. In the evening, relax in the Banff Upper Hot Springs.

Day 6: Icefields Parkway

Drive along the Icefields Parkway to Jasper. Stop at the Columbia Icefield and take a tour on the Athabasca Glacier. Continue to Jasper and check into your hotel.

Day 7: Jasper National Park

Explore Jasper National Park. Visit Maligne Lake and take a boat cruise to Spirit Island. Discover the beauty of Maligne Canyon and relax in the tranquil surroundings of the park. In the evening, enjoy stargazing in Jasper, a designated Dark Sky Preserve.

Day 8: Jasper to Calgary

Drive back to Calgary. Along the way, stop at the picturesque Peyto Lake and Bow Lake. Arrive in Calgary, check into your hotel, and explore the city's vibrant downtown area.

Day 9: Calgary

Discover Calgary's attractions. Visit the Calgary Tower for a panoramic view of the city, explore the Glenbow Museum, and stroll through the Calgary Zoo. In the evening, enjoy a dinner at one of Calgary's top restaurants.

Day 10: Departure from Calgary

Spend your final morning in Calgary. Visit the Heritage Park Historical Village or do some last-minute shopping at the CORE Shopping Centre. Depart from Calgary in the afternoon.
