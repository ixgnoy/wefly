Brazil : 8 Days, 7 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Rio de Janeiro

Arrive in Rio de Janeiro and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the iconic Christ the Redeemer statue and enjoy panoramic views of the city. In the evening, relax at Copacabana Beach and dine at a local churrascaria.

Day 2: Rio de Janeiro

Discover the highlights of Rio. Visit Sugarloaf Mountain in the morning for stunning views. In the afternoon, explore the historic neighborhood of Santa Teresa and visit the Selarón Steps. In the evening, enjoy a samba show in Lapa.

Day 3: Tijuca Forest and Botanical Garden

Spend the day exploring the natural beauty of Rio. Take a jeep tour of Tijuca National Park, one of the world's largest urban rainforests. In the afternoon, visit the Rio de Janeiro Botanical Garden. In the evening, explore the nightlife of Ipanema.

Day 4: Rio de Janeiro to Foz do Iguaçu

Fly to Foz do Iguaçu. Check into your hotel and visit the Brazilian side of Iguaçu Falls. Take a walking tour of the falls and enjoy the stunning views. In the evening, relax at your hotel or explore the town.

Day 5: Iguaçu Falls

Visit the Argentine side of Iguaçu Falls for a different perspective. Take the train to the Devil's Throat and explore the various trails. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Argentine dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 6: Foz do Iguaçu to Salvador

Fly to Salvador, the vibrant capital of Bahia. Check into your hotel and explore the historic Pelourinho district, known for its colorful colonial buildings and lively atmosphere. In the evening, enjoy a Bahian dinner and a performance of traditional Afro-Brazilian music and dance.

Day 7: Salvador

Discover the cultural heritage of Salvador. Visit the São Francisco Church and Convent, explore the Mercado Modelo, and take a stroll along the Barra Lighthouse. Spend the afternoon relaxing on one of Salvador's beautiful beaches. In the evening, enjoy a seafood dinner in Rio Vermelho.

Day 8: Departure from Salvador

Spend your final morning in Salvador. Visit the Bonfim Church or take a capoeira class. Do some last-minute shopping for souvenirs before departing from Salvador in the afternoon.
