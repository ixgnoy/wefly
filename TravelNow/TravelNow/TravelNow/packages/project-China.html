China: 7 Days, 6 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Beijing

Arrive in Beijing and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to Tiananmen Square and the Forbidden City. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Peking duck dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Beijing

Discover the historical and cultural landmarks of Beijing. Visit the Temple of Heaven in the morning. Then, head to the Summer Palace for a leisurely afternoon. In the evening, enjoy a Kung Fu show or an acrobatics performance.

Day 3: Great Wall of China

Take a day trip to the Great Wall of China at Mutianyu. Enjoy a hike along the wall and take in the breathtaking views. Return to Beijing in the evening and explore the Wangfujing Night Market for local snacks and souvenirs.

Day 4: Beijing to Xi'an

Fly to Xi'an, the ancient capital of China. Check into your hotel and visit the ancient City Wall. In the evening, explore the Muslim Quarter, famous for its vibrant street food scene.

Day 5: Xi'an

Spend the day visiting the Terracotta Army, one of the most significant archaeological discoveries in the world. Afterward, visit the Big Wild Goose Pagoda. In the evening, enjoy a Tang Dynasty show with a dumpling banquet.

Day 6: Xi'an to Shanghai

Fly to Shanghai, the modern metropolis of China. Check into your hotel and visit the Bund, a famous waterfront area. Explore Nanjing Road for shopping and dining. In the evening, take a Huangpu River cruise to see the city's skyline illuminated.

Day 7: Shanghai

Visit the Yu Garden and the adjacent Yuyuan Bazaar. Explore the Jade Buddha Temple and the Shanghai Museum. Spend the afternoon at the Shanghai Tower for a panoramic view of the city. Enjoy your final dinner in the French Concession area, known for its charming atmosphere and excellent cuisine. Depart from Shanghai in the evening.
