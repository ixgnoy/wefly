UK : 10 Days, 9 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in London

Arrive in London and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Tower of London and Tower Bridge. In the evening, enjoy dinner at a traditional British pub.

Day 2: London

Discover the highlights of London. Visit Buckingham Palace in the morning to see the Changing of the Guard. Explore Westminster Abbey and the Houses of Parliament. In the evening, enjoy a West End theatre show.

Day 3: London

Spend the day visiting more of London's attractions. Visit the British Museum and stroll through Covent Garden. In the afternoon, take a ride on the London Eye for a panoramic view of the city. In the evening, explore the South Bank and dine at a riverside restaurant.

Day 4: London to Bath

Take a train to Bath. Check into your hotel and visit the Roman Baths and Bath Abbey. In the afternoon, stroll through the Royal Crescent and the Circus. Enjoy a relaxing evening at a local restaurant.

Day 5: Bath

Spend the day exploring Bath. Visit the Jane Austen Centre and the Fashion Museum. Take a scenic walk along the Kennet and Avon Canal. In the evening, enjoy a dinner cruise on the River Avon.

Day 6: Bath to the Cotswolds

Travel to the Cotswolds. Explore charming villages such as Castle Combe and Bibury. Visit the Cotswold Wildlife Park or take a leisurely hike through the countryside. Check into a cozy inn and enjoy a traditional English dinner.

Day 7: Cotswolds to Stratford-upon-Avon

Travel to Stratford-upon-Avon, the birthplace of William Shakespeare. Check into your hotel and visit Shakespeare's Birthplace and Anne Hathaway's Cottage. In the evening, enjoy a performance by the Royal Shakespeare Company.

Day 8: Stratford-upon-Avon to York

Take a train to York. Check into your hotel and explore the medieval city. Visit York Minster and take a walk along the city walls. In the evening, explore the Shambles and dine at a historic inn.

Day 9: York

Spend the day in York. Visit the National Railway Museum and the Jorvik Viking Centre. Take a boat trip on the River Ouse and explore the York Castle Museum. In the evening, enjoy a ghost tour of the city.

Day 10: Departure from London

Return to London for your final day. Visit any remaining sights or do some last-minute shopping at Harrods or Oxford Street. Depart from London in the afternoon.
