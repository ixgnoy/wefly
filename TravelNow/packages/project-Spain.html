Spain: 11 Days, 10 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Barcelona

Arrive in Barcelona and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Sagrada Familia and Park Güell, iconic landmarks designed by Antoni Gaudí. Stroll along La Rambla and enjoy a dinner of tapas at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Barcelona

Discover the cultural and architectural wonders of Barcelona. Visit the Picasso Museum, Gothic Quarter, and Barcelona Cathedral. In the evening, take a leisurely walk along the beachfront or enjoy a flamenco show.

Day 3: Barcelona to Valencia

Travel to Valencia by high-speed train. Check into your hotel and explore the City of Arts and Sciences, including the Oceanografic and Hemisfèric. Visit the historic Valencia Cathedral and Mercado Central. Enjoy a traditional Valencian paella dinner.

Day 4: Valencia to Granada

Travel to Granada and check into your hotel. Visit the Alhambra Palace, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and Generalife Gardens. Explore the historic Albayzin district and enjoy views of the Alhambra from Mirador de San Nicolás.

Day 5: Granada

Explore more of Granada, visiting the Cathedral and Royal Chapel. Take a leisurely walk through the Albaicín neighborhood and enjoy a traditional Andalusian dinner with views of the Alhambra.

Day 6: Granada to Seville

Travel to Seville and check into your hotel. Visit the Seville Cathedral, Giralda Tower, and the Royal Alcázar of Seville. Explore the historic Santa Cruz neighborhood and enjoy a flamenco performance in the evening.

Day 7: Seville

Take a day trip to the ancient city of Córdoba. Visit the Mezquita-Cathedral, a unique blend of Moorish and Christian architecture. Explore the Jewish Quarter and the Alcázar de los Reyes Cristianos. Return to Seville in the evening.

Day 8: Seville to Madrid

Travel to Madrid by high-speed train. Check into your hotel and visit the Prado Museum, home to works by Velázquez, Goya, and El Greco. Explore Retiro Park and Puerta del Sol. Enjoy a traditional Spanish dinner in the lively neighborhood of La Latina.

Day 9: Madrid

Discover more of Madrid's cultural treasures. Visit the Royal Palace, Plaza Mayor, and the Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum. Explore the lively Gran Vía and enjoy a sunset view from the Temple of Debod.

Day 10: Madrid to Toledo

Take a day trip to Toledo, known as the City of Three Cultures. Explore the Toledo Cathedral, Synagogue of Santa María la Blanca, and the Alcázar of Toledo. Return to Madrid in the evening.

Day 11: Departure from Madrid

Spend your final morning in Madrid shopping for souvenirs at the El Rastro Market or exploring any last-minute sights. Head to the airport for your departure, taking with you memories of Spain's rich history, culture, and cuisine.
