India : 6 Days, 5 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Dehli

Arrive in Dehli and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to India Gate and the bustling markets of Connaught Place. Enjoy a traditional Indian dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Delhi

Discover the historical and cultural landmarks of Delhi. Visit the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. Explore Chandni Chowk and take a rickshaw ride through its narrow lanes. In the evening, witness the vibrant sound and light show at the Red Fort.

Day 3: Delhi to Agra

Travel to Agra, home of the iconic Taj Mahal. Check into your hotel and visit the Agra Fort and Itimad-ud-Daulah's Tomb (Baby Taj). In the late afternoon, marvel at the beauty of the Taj Mahal during sunset. Enjoy a Mughlai dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 4: Agra to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri

Drive to Jaipur, stopping en route at Fatehpur Sikri, the abandoned Mughal city. Explore the Buland Darwaza, Panch Mahal, and other historical sites. Continue your journey to Jaipur, the Pink City, and check into your hotel. Spend the evening at leisure.

Day 5: Jaipur

Explore the royal heritage of Jaipur.Visit the Majestic Amber Fort,Hawa Mahal(Palace of Winds), and City Palace.Discover the Jantar Mantar observatory. In the evening,enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner with folk dance performances at Chokhi Dhani.

Day 6: Departure from Jaipur

Spend your final morning in Jaipur shopping for souvenirs at local markets like Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar. Visit the Albert Hall Museum if time permits. Afterward, head to the airport for your departure.
