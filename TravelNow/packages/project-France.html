France: 10 Days, 9 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Paris

Arrive in Paris and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Eiffel Tower and enjoy panoramic views of the city. Stroll along the Seine River and have a French dinner at a local bistro.

Day 2: Paris

Discover the cultural and artistic wonders of Paris. Visit the Louvre Museum, home to the Mona Lisa and many other masterpieces. Explore the Notre-Dame Cathedral and wander through the historic Latin Quarter. In the evening, take a boat cruise on the Seine.

Day 3: Paris

Continue exploring Paris. Visit the Musée d'Orsay, known for its Impressionist art collection. Explore Montmartre and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica. Enjoy dinner at a traditional Parisian brasserie.

Day 4: Paris to Bordeaux

Travel to Bordeaux by train. Check into your hotel and visit the Bordeaux Wine Museum. Explore the historic city center, including the Place de la Bourse and the Bordeaux Cathedral. Enjoy a dinner of local specialties.

Day 5: Bordeaux to Lyon

Travel to Lyon and check into your hotel. Visit the Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière and enjoy panoramic views of Lyon. Explore Vieux Lyon and its traboules (hidden passageways). Have dinner in a typical Lyonnais bouchon.

Day 6: Lyon

Visit the Musée des Beaux-Arts de Lyon, one of France's largest fine arts museums. Explore the Croix-Rousse neighborhood and its silk workshops. In the evening, enjoy a dinner cruise along the Rhône River.

Day 7: Lyon to Nice

Travel to Nice and check into your hotel. Visit the Promenade des Anglais and the Old Town (Vieux Nice). Explore the Cours Saleya Market and enjoy a traditional Niçois lunch. Relax on the beach or take a stroll along the waterfront.

Day 8: Nice to Cannes and Monaco

Take a day trip to Cannes, known for its film festival, and Monaco, home to the famous Monte Carlo Casino. Visit the Prince's Palace of Monaco and the Oceanographic Museum. Return to Nice in the evening.

Day 9: Nice to Avignon

Travel to Avignon and check into your hotel. Visit the Palais des Papes (Popes' Palace) and the Pont d'Avignon. Explore the historic center and enjoy dinner at a Provençal restaurant.

Day 10: Avignon to Paris

Return to Paris by train. Spend your final day in Paris shopping along the Champs-Élysées or visiting any last-minute sights. Enjoy a farewell dinner at a gourmet restaurant.

Day 11: Departure from Paris

Spend your final morning in Paris at leisure, perhaps visiting a museum or a park. Head to the airport for your departure, taking with you memories of France's rich history, culture, and cuisine.
