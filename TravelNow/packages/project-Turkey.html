Turkey: 7 Days, 6 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Istanbul

Arrive in Istanbul and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Hagia Sophia and the Blue Mosque. Stroll through the historic Sultanahmet district. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Turkish dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Istanbul

Discover the rich history of Istanbul. Visit Topkapi Palace and the Basilica Cistern. Explore the Grand Bazaar for shopping and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere. In the evening, take a Bosphorus dinner cruise to see the city illuminated by night.

Day 3: Istanbul to Cappadocia

Fly to Cappadocia and check into your cave hotel. Visit the Göreme Open-Air Museum and the ancient rock formations. Take a walk through the fairy chimneys of Pasabag (Monk's Valley). In the evening, enjoy a traditional Anatolian dinner.

Day 4: Cappadocia

Start the day with a hot air balloon ride over Cappadocia’s unique landscape. Visit the underground city of Derinkuyu or Kaymakli. Explore the beautiful valleys such as Love Valley and Pigeon Valley. End the day with a sunset viewpoint.

Day 5: Cappadocia to Pamukkale

Fly to Denizli and transfer to Pamukkale. Check into your hotel and visit the stunning travertine terraces. Explore the ancient city of Hierapolis, including its theater and the Necropolis. Relax in the thermal waters of the Cleopatra Pool.

Day 6: Pamukkale to Ephesus to Izmir

Drive to Ephesus and explore the well-preserved ruins, including the Library of Celsus and the Great Theatre. Visit the House of the Virgin Mary. Continue to Izmir and check into your hotel. Spend the evening at leisure exploring the coastal city.

Day 7: Izmir to Istanbul

Fly back to Istanbul. Spend your final day visiting the Spice Bazaar and the Dolmabahce Palace. Take a stroll along Istiklal Street and visit the Galata Tower for a panoramic view of the city. Enjoy a farewell dinner in the bustling district of Beyoglu before departing from Istanbul in the evening.
