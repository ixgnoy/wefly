USA: 9 Days 8 Nights

Day 1-2: Arrival in New York City

Day 1

Arrive in the bustling metropolis of New York City and settle into your hotel in the heart of Manhattan. Dive into the vibrant energy of Times Square, where neon lights dazzle and Broadway shows beckon. Take a leisurely stroll through Central Park, an oasis of green amidst the urban jungle.

Explore the iconic landmarks of NYC such as the Statue of Liberty, Empire State Building, and the solemn beauty of the 9/11 Memorial. Conclude your day with a culinary adventure through diverse neighborhoods, savoring the flavors of the city.

Day 3: New York City to Yellowstone National Park

Embark on a journey to the untamed wilderness of Yellowstone National Park. Fly into Bozeman, Montana, and drive to the park, marveling at the rugged beauty of the Rocky Mountains along the way.

Day 4-5: Yellowstone National Park

Discover the geothermal wonders of Yellowstone, from the iconic Old Faithful geyser to the colorful Grand Prismatic Spring. Keep your eyes peeled for wildlife sightings, including bison, elk, and grizzly bears.

Day 6: Yellowstone to Denali National Park

Travel north to Alaska and venture into the pristine wilderness of Denali National Park. Enjoy a scenic flightseeing tour over the towering peaks of the Alaska Range, with a chance to spot elusive wildlife like wolves and moose.

Day 7-8: Denali National Park

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking landscapes of Denali, with opportunities for hiking, wildlife photography, and cultural experiences with the native Athabascan people. As night falls, keep an eye out for the mesmerizing Northern Lights dancing across the Arctic sky.

Day 9: Seattle

Spend your final day in Seattle indulging in fresh seafood at waterfront restaurants, taking a scenic ferry ride to nearby islands, or exploring the city's thriving arts and music scene. Bid farewell to the Pacific Northwest, taking with you memories of the USA's diverse landscapes and cultural riches.
