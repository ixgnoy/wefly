Switzerland : 10 Days, 9 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Zurich

Arrive in Zurich and check into your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Old Town (Altstadt) and the Bahnhofstrasse. In the evening, enjoy dinner at a local Swiss restaurant.

Day 2: Zurich

Discover the highlights of Zurich. Visit the Swiss National Museum in the morning, then take a stroll along Lake Zurich. In the afternoon, visit the Zurich Zoo or the Kunsthaus Zurich art museum. In the evening, enjoy a performance at the Zurich Opera House.

Day 3: Zurich to Lucerne

Take a train to Lucerne. Explore the Chapel Bridge (Kapellbrücke) and the Water Tower. Visit the Lion Monument and take a leisurely walk along the lake. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Swiss dinner at a lakeside restaurant.

Day 4: Lucerne

Spend the day in Lucerne. Take a boat trip on Lake Lucerne and enjoy the stunning views. In the afternoon, visit the Swiss Transport Museum or take a cable car to Mount Pilatus for a panoramic view of the Swiss Alps. Return to Lucerne for the night.

Day 5: Lucerne to Interlaken

Take a scenic train ride to Interlaken. Check into your hotel and explore the town nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. In the afternoon, take a funicular to Harder Kulm for breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and lakes. In the evening, dine at a local restaurant.

Day 6: Jungfrau Region

Take a day trip to the Jungfrau Region. Visit Jungfraujoch, the "Top of Europe," via a cogwheel train. Enjoy the snow-capped peaks and ice sculptures in the Ice Palace. Return to Interlaken in the evening.

Day 7: Interlaken to Zermatt

Take a train to Zermatt, home of the Matterhorn. Check into your hotel and explore the charming car-free village. In the afternoon, take the Gornergrat Railway for spectacular views of the Matterhorn. In the evening, enjoy a Swiss fondue dinner.

Day 8: Zermatt

Spend the day exploring Zermatt. Take a cable car to Klein Matterhorn for a breathtaking view of the Alps. Visit the Matterhorn Museum and learn about the history of the region. In the evening, relax and enjoy the peaceful mountain atmosphere.

Day 9: Zermatt to Montreux

Take a train to Montreux along the scenic GoldenPass Line. Check into your hotel and stroll along the beautiful Montreux Riviera. Visit Chillon Castle, one of Switzerland's most iconic landmarks. In the evening, enjoy a lakeside dinner.

Day 10: Departure from Geneva

Travel to Geneva for your final day. Visit the Jet d'Eau and the United Nations headquarters. Explore the Old Town and the St. Pierre Cathedral. Depart from Geneva in the afternoon.
