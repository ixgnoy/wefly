Singapore: 3 Days, 2 Nights

Day 1: Arrival in Singapore

Arrive in Singapore, check into your hotel. Visit Marina Bay Sands and the Gardens by the Bay in the evening. Watch the Supertree Grove light show.

Day 2: Singapore

Spend the morning at Sentosa Island, enjoying attractions like Universal Studios Singapore and S.E.A. Aquarium. In the afternoon, visit Chinatown, Little India, and Haji Lane for cultural experiences and shopping. End the day at Clarke Quay for dining and nightlife.

Day 3: Departure from Singapore

Visit the Singapore Zoo or River Safari in the morning. Explore Orchard Road for shopping in the afternoon. Depart from Singapore in the evening.
